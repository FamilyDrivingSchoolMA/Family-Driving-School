Our Policies

What You Need to Know

Please review our policies before booking. These guidelines help us provide a reliable, professional experience for every student.

Behind the Wheel

Lessons may be paid per session or paid in full upfront for a set number of classes. Lessons cannot be paid for after the fact.
Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before your scheduled appointment. Failure to do so will result in a $30 cancellation fee before another appointment can be booked.
All appointments must be confirmed with a pick-up location to verify your time slot.
A $3 processing fee will be added to any payment made through third-party banking apps.

Road Test

Before scheduling a road test through Family Driving School Inc., all payments — including classroom, behind-the-wheel, sponsorship, and road test registration fees — must be up to date.
Cancellation of any scheduled road test must be made 72 hours before the set date to ensure proper communication between all parties.
⚠ Failure to attend a scheduled road test will result in the forfeiture of all road test fees, as payments are made directly to the RMV for our sanctioned road tests.
